# Audio Files for Check-In

## success-beep.mp3
Place a short (1-2 second) success sound effect here.

You can:
1. Download from freesound.org (search "success beep")
2. Use online generators like sfxr.me
3. Record a simple "ding" sound

**Requirements:**
- Format: MP3
- Duration: 1-2 seconds
- Volume: Medium (not too loud)
- Tone: Pleasant, positive

**Example search:** "notification success short beep"
